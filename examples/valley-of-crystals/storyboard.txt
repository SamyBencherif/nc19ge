
You have some basic skillsets (think Fallout 3)

Friendly

You are trustworthy and relatable. Characters will befriend you more
easily.

Swiftness

You are quick to your wits. You have enhanced stealth abilities and
are advantaged at skills that require intellect--such as diciphering
codes.

Bargaining

You always manage a good deal. Trades tend to lean to your favor.

Courage

You're what they call 'courageous'. You have an advantage during
battles and standoffs.

Enchanting

You are magically gifted. You have special advantage at skills that
use magic.
